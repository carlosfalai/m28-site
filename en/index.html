Services

  • STBBI (STI) screening — chlamydia, gonorrhea, syphilis, HIV, hepatitis, herpes; samples collected at your local CLSC laboratory (page in French).

How the consultation works

  1. Telemedicine consultation — answer the physician's questions through secure messaging.

  2. If needed, a laboratory requisition — samples are collected at your local CLSC, at no charge.

  3. Results are explained and provided as a PDF.

  4. The physician explains your results and the recommended course of action. Follow-up by messaging at any time, every day.

Telemedicine is not suitable for every situation. When a physical examination is necessary, or when your condition cannot be assessed remotely, an in-person examination will be recommended.
